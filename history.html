2018 | The Beginning

A hub built for first-time founders

HHC Franchise Hub Solutions Corp. was born from a simple goal: give aspiring Filipino entrepreneurs an accessible, profitable, and well-supported path to ownership. The first playbooks, partners, and processes were designed to remove guesswork from starting up.

From one concept to a portfolio

As trust in the model grew, so did the roster--food, wellness, and digital service franchises were added to meet different budgets and markets. Playbooks were refreshed for mall corridors, neighborhood hubs, and delivery-first locations.
