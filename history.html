2018 | The Beginning

A hub built for first-time founders

HHC Franchise Hub Solutions Corp. was born from a simple goal: give aspiring Filipino entrepreneurs an accessible, profitable, and well-supported path to ownership. The first playbooks, partners, and processes were designed to remove guesswork from starting up.

First batch of kiosks Supplier network locked in
Board-led expansion

2020 | Growth & Expansion

From playbook to powerhouse

The board sharpened the rollout blueprint, adding governance, capital partners, and a unified brand system. Food, wellness, and digital services scaled together with tighter supply lines and mall-to-neighborhood formats.

Board stewardship Multi-format rollout Unified brand system
Momentum in motion

2023 | Making Impact

Taking the brand nationwide

The team went mobile, opening in airports, transport hubs, and provincial downtowns while pushing digital channels. Franchisees gained faster approvals, drone site surveys, and launch squads that hit revenue targets sooner.

Launch squads Omnichannel rollout Field intelligence
Ops & experience lab

2026 | Today & Beyond

Designing the next wave

An operations lab now pilots experience upgrades, sustainability standards, and data-led merchandising. Always-on coaching, AI-assisted demand forecasts, and loyalty loops keep franchisees resilient and ahead of the curve.
